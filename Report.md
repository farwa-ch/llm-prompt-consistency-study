# Analysis of Prompt Sensitivity on Responses of Large Language Models

## Abstract
The current work examines how changes in prompts influence the consistency of outputs generated by large language models.

## Introduction
LLMs are highly effective, but their outputs can differ when fed with prompts that have slight differences. 

## Methods
- Data: 50 questions 
- Types of prompts: baseline, paraphrase, contextual, adversarial, structured
- Analysis: cosine similarity and manual inspection

## Results
- Consistency observed with basic questions
- Variability noted with reasoning tasks
- Instability induced by adversarial prompts

## Conclusion
Prompting strategies can greatly affect LLMs’ responses.
